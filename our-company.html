THE LIFEWOOD DIFFERENCE

CONTACT US

At Lifewood, we connect with the thousands of people

who work with us in countries often described as

'developing economies' and we realise that we are being

offered the chance to provide opportunities.

 

Opportunities for employment with wages high enough

to change people's lives. Opportunities to teach and

provide computing and tech-skills equal to anywhere in

the world, making our people the breadwinners in their families.

For instance, in Bangladesh we employ a high proportion

of women and even more surprisingly, the physically

disabled. We have been especially inspired by the

example of Tilotom, the eldest son of a low-income

family. Physically disabled since birth, he's now the

family's primary breadwinner, thanks to Lifewood's

training and his determination to overcome all obstacles.

He has quickly progressed to become one of the

company's best performers, managing a large portfolio of

responsibilities.
